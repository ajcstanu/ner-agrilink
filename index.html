Transport Modes

Four Pathways to Market

Purpose-built for NER's hills, valleys, and forest corridors

🚡

Smart Cable Conveyor

Aerial solar + battery–powered cableway with IoT cargo monitoring. Ideal for steep hilly terrain with no road access.

  • 🔋 Solar + battery hybrid
  • 📡 Real-time load monitoring
  • 📱 Mobile app booking
  • 💰 ₹0.80/kg operational cost
Best for: 45°+ slopes, 500m+ elevation
🚝

Lightweight Monorail

Cableway-type monorail on concrete poles, running trolleys from fields to collection points with only 2 operators.

  • 🏗️ Concrete pole structure
  • 👥 2-operator system
  • 📦 500 kg per trip capacity
  • 💰 ₹1.20/kg operational cost
Best for: Uneven mid-slope terrain
🛺

Solar/Electric Cargo Trike

Rugged 3-wheeler with solar charging and modular detachable crates. GPS-tracked for transparent logistics on narrow paths.

  • ☀️ Solar-charged battery
  • 📍 GPS real-time tracking
  • 🧩 Modular crate system
  • 💰 ₹1.50/kg operational cost
Best for: Narrow rural pathways, <25° slope
🎋

Bamboo-Based Carrier

Carriages built from local Bambusa Balcoa — lightweight, durable, zero import cost. Weather-protected with plastic covers.

  • 🌿 100% local material
  • 🛡️ Weather-proof covers
  • 🏔️ Steep terrain capable
  • 💰 ₹0.60/kg operational cost
Best for: Remote villages, zero-infra zones

Why It Works for NER

🌱

Reduced Spoilage

Faster, reliable transport cuts post-harvest losses by up to 40% compared to manual head-loading over steep terrain.

💰

Higher ROI for Farmers

Operational costs kept at ₹1–2/kg. Farmers reinvest savings, improving livelihoods across remote NE communities.

☀️

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable

Solar-powered systems reduce fossil fuel dependency in ecologically sensitive NER zones and tribal forests.

📶

IoT Transparency

Real-time monitoring of load, temperature, and route ensures accountability and trust between farmers and buyers.

🏔️

Terrain-Adaptive Design

Multiple modes (cable, trike, bamboo) ensure every farm — regardless of slope or remoteness — can access logistics.

Local Material Use
